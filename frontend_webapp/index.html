How it works

Intelligence in three steps.

Works for gadgets and flights. Paste a screenshot, type a query, or just speak.

Step 1
📋
Paste your WhatsApp screenshot
Got a deal forwarded on WhatsApp? Paste the listing screenshot — or type the product name. Nova Lite reads the text and identifies the exact model with 96%+ accuracy.
Step 2
⚡
AI searches Amazon & Flipkart
Two Nova Act agents search Amazon.in and Flipkart simultaneously — in parallel, not one after the other. Live prices, card offers, coupon codes and delivery costs, all in ~30 seconds.
Step 3
🏆
Voice announces the winner
Nova Sonic tells you the true cheapest price after your card offer is applied. Tap once to buy — FareWise opens the exact product page with the price locked in.
Step 1
🎤

Card discount applied
Nova Pro calculates the effective fare for each platform × card combination — instant discounts, cashback, promo codes — and announces the cheapest booking option.

Powered by Amazon Nova

Five Nova models. One decision.

Every search chains five specialised Amazon Nova models in sequence — each handling what it does best.

👁
Nova Lite
Product Identifier
Reads text labels from WhatsApp screenshots to extract exact model number, brand and variant — no pre-built catalog needed. 96%+ accuracy on listing images.
🔗
Nova Multimodal
SKU Validator
Embeds your product image and search result thumbnails into a shared vector space. Cosine similarity confirms the right product is being priced — not a lookalike.
🌐
Nova Act
Web Agents (×2–3, parallel)
Autonomous browser agents that navigate Amazon, Flipkart, MakeMyTrip, Goibibo and Cleartrip simultaneously — server-side, no extra permissions needed on OTA sites.
🧮
Nova Pro
Price Reasoner
Multi-variable reasoning across platform price × card cashback × EMI discount × reward points × delivery cost to find the true effective price — in plain language.
🎙
Nova Sonic
Voice Interface (bidirectional)
Parses spoken routes ("Mumbai to Delhi, this Friday, economy") and announces the winner with barge-in support — so you can ask a follow-up before it finishes speaking. Works in Indian English with ₹ pronunciation.
