# CLEARTRIP FLIGHT RESULTS PAGE

Flight search results appear as a vertical list of repeating flight cards
in the main results container.

Focus only on the flight cards.
Ignore navigation, headers, ads, banners, sidebars, and footers.

Each flight card contains: airline name, flight number, departure time,
arrival time, duration, stops, and price.

## STRICT RULES

This is a DATA EXTRACTION task.

- Do NOT click any buttons, links, "Book", "View Details", or flight cards.
- Do NOT open popups, modals, or fare breakdowns.
- Do NOT click sorting or filtering controls on the page.
- Only READ visible card data from the DOM.
