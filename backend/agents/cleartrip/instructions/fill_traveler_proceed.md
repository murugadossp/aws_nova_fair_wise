## DISMISS POPUPS

If any popup, overlay, or dialog is blocking the page (coupon offers, login prompts, price alerts, fare change notices), dismiss it first by clicking "OK", "Close", the X button, or clicking outside the popup.

## FILL TRAVELER DETAILS

On the current booking/itinerary page, scroll down the main content area to find the traveler details form and contact details form. These may appear on the same page or across multiple steps.

Fill in the following values wherever the fields appear:

- Title / Gender: Select "Mr" or "Male"
- First Name: Test
- Last Name: User
- Mobile / Phone: 1234567890
- Email: test@farewise.com

If any field is pre-filled or disabled, leave it as-is.
After filling all visible required fields, click the "Continue" button to proceed.

## SKIP ADD-ONS

After submitting traveler details, one or more add-on screens may appear (seats, meals, baggage, insurance, or similar upsells).

For every add-on screen:
- Do NOT select any add-on.
- Click "Skip", "Skip add-ons", "Skip add ons", "No thanks", "Continue without", or the equivalent skip/continue button.
- If a confirmation popup appears asking to confirm skipping, click "Skip" or "Skip add ons" again.
- If there is no explicit skip button, click "Continue" or "Proceed".

Keep skipping and filling details until you reach the final payment or checkout page (the page with payment options like credit card, UPI, etc.).
Do NOT enter any payment details — just land on the page.
