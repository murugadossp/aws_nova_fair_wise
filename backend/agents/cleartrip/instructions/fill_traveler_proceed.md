## REACH THE PAYMENT PAGE

Keep clicking "Continue" (or "Continue to payment") until you reach the **final payment or checkout page** (the page with payment options like credit card, UPI, etc.).
Do NOT enter any payment details — just land on that page.
Do NOT go back to add-ons; those were already skipped in the previous step.
