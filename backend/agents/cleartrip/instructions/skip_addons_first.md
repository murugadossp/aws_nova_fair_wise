## DISMISS POPUPS

If any popup, overlay, or dialog is blocking the page (coupon offers, login prompts, price alerts), dismiss it first by clicking "OK", "Close", the X button, or clicking outside the popup.

## SKIP ADD-ONS (FIRST SCREEN)

You are on or will see the **"Choose add-ons"** screen (seats, meals, baggage, insurance, or similar upsells). This is the **first** step before contact and traveler details.

- Do NOT fill any contact or traveler form yet.
- Do NOT select any add-on.
- Click "Skip", "Skip add-ons", "Skip add ons", "No thanks", "Continue without", or the equivalent button to skip this screen.
- If a confirmation popup appears (e.g. "You missed adding Meal(s) and Seat(s)"), click "Skip add ons" or "Skip" again.
- If there are multiple add-on screens, keep clicking Skip/Continue until you see the **contact details** or **traveler details** form (phone, email, name fields).

Your task is complete when the add-ons screen(s) are skipped and the page shows contact/traveler fields. Do not fill any form in this step.
